Before embarking on your aventure you must first determine your strengths and weaknesses.
If u want to determine your strengths turn to 3 or turn to 7 to determine your weaknesses

How to determine your strengths:
If your attack strength is higher, you have wounded the creature. If the creature's attack strength
is higher has wounded you.

You must now either leave (turn to 9), or you may attack him (turn to 119).
